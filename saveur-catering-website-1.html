Common Questions

Answers Before
You Ask

Everything you need to know before reaching out — and if your question isn't here, we're one message away.

For weddings, we recommend booking 3–6 months in advance, especially for peak wedding season (October–February). For corporate events and birthday parties, 4–8 weeks is usually sufficient. We'll always check availability for your date first.

Our minimum is 30 guests for most events and 50 guests for full wedding packages. We have successfully catered events with 2,000+ guests with full live station setups. No event is too large or too intimate.

Absolutely. We specialise in fully customized menus for every dietary requirement — Jain, vegan, vegetarian, gluten-free, Keto, diabetic-friendly. We use separate equipment and service protocols to prevent cross-contamination.

Yes — we offer complimentary tasting sessions for wedding bookings and large events (200+ guests). For smaller events, a tasting is available at a nominal cost that is fully adjusted against your final invoice if you proceed.

Our packages include food preparation, service staff, chafing dishes and buffet equipment, live station setup, and post-event cleanup. Décor and dedicated event coordination are included in Gold and Platinum packages. All quotes include a full itemised breakdown — no hidden charges, ever.

Yes — outdoor and destination catering is one of our specialities. We bring our own generators, cooking equipment, refrigeration, and service setup. We've catered at farmhouses, resorts, rooftops, and remote venues. Distance surcharges may apply outside our primary service area.

Cancellations 30+ days before your event receive a full refund of the advance. Cancellations within 15–30 days receive a 50% refund. Within 15 days, the advance is non-refundable but can be applied to a future booking within 12 months.
